## Capture settings

- **Format:** PNG
- **Aspect:** Whatever fits the moment naturally — full-window screenshots are usually fine
- **Width:** ~1200–1600px ideal (GitHub renders nicely at this size)
- **Annotations:** Optional. If you want to highlight specific buttons, draw a thin red rectangle around the target element. Don't over-annotate.

## How to capture

**macOS:** `Cmd + Shift + 4` then drag a region. Press `Space` while dragging to capture a window.

**Windows:** `Win + Shift + S` then drag a region. The capture lands in your clipboard — paste into Preview / Paint / Photoshop and save as PNG.
