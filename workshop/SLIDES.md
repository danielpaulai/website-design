## How to use this with Gamma.app

1. Open **<https://gamma.app>** and click **Create new** → **Generate**
2. Choose **Paste in text** (not "Generate from prompt")
3. Copy the **Outline for Gamma** section below verbatim
4. Choose a **dark / luxury** theme — closest match: "Oasis", "Atlas", or "Marble" (dark variants)
5. Set image style to: **Editorial photography** or **Cinematic**
6. Generate. Then refine slide-by-slide using the **Detailed slide-by-slide** section below.

If Gamma offers an "outline-first" mode, use it — that gives you the most control before image generation kicks in.

## Bonus — Speaker preparation tips

- **Read the deck out loud once** before the session. The cadence is built around the story rhythm. The Ice Cube slide especially — slow it down.
- **The room rules slide is when energy can sag.** Move quickly. Don't preach.
- **Slide 13 is your identity-shift moment.** Slow down. Make eye contact. Let the silence between sentences land.
- **Slide 15 is your transition into work mode.** Say the line, then literally turn to your screen and open Claude Code. The room will follow.
